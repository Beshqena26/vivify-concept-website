Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services and work process.

General Questions

We offer comprehensive marketing solutions: ATL digital marketing, BTL retail marketing, and full HORECA project management — strategy development, branding, social media, content, photo/video production, and more.

We work across diverse industries: hospitality (HORECA), retail, food and beverage, tourism, and lifestyle brands. Our team tailors strategies to each industry's unique needs.

We are based in Georgia and serve clients across the entire region. We work with both local and international brands.

Working With Us

Book a free 30-minute strategic consultation where we'll discuss your brand's goals, challenges, and opportunities. We'll provide initial recommendations. No obligations.

Project timelines vary by scope. A branding project takes 2-4 weeks, social media management is monthly, and HORECA projects take 1-3 months depending on complexity. We'll provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.

Our process includes four stages: (1) Discovery — analysis of the brand, market, and competition; (2) Strategy — creating a tailored marketing plan; (3) Execution — bringing the strategy to life with creative campaigns; (4) Results — measuring outcomes, optimization, and reporting.

Yes, we offer flexible monthly packages for social media management, content creation, and marketing strategy services. This ensures consistent brand representation.

Pricing & Payment

Our pricing is project-based and tailored to your specific needs. We provide transparent quotes during the consultation stage. No hidden fees — you always know exactly what you're paying for.

We accept bank transfers, card payments, and can arrange flexible payment schedules for larger projects. Payment terms are agreed upon before the project begins.

Results & Reporting

We use data-driven metrics: engagement rates, reach, conversions, ROI, brand awareness growth, and custom KPIs aligned with your business goals.

For ongoing projects, we provide comprehensive monthly reports. For campaign-based projects, we prepare detailed post-analysis with actionable recommendations.
