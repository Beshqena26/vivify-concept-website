01 — What is BTL

Targeted Marketing at the Point of Sale

Below The Line (BTL) marketing includes targeted activities directly at the point of sale — in retail stores, supermarkets, and shopping spaces. It is direct communication with consumers at the moment of purchase.

We manage your brand's representation in retail — from competitor analysis to promo activities. Our team ensures that your product is always visible, accessible, and competitive on the shelf.
