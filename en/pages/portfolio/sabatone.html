Restaurant Design and Layout with Full Technical Documentation

Sabatone restaurant needed a complete space redesign and functional layout. The existing space was being used inefficiently, guest seating capacity was low, and the kitchen zoning didn't meet modern HORECA standards. Space optimization was needed considering customer comfort and operational efficiency.

We conducted a full site measurement, created technical drawings and furniture layout plans. We designed functional zoning: kitchen, dining hall, bar area, and service corridors. Each zone was optimized for maximum workflow and guest experience efficiency.
