BTL Product Positioning and Shelf Placement Strategy

The Ojakhuri brand needed to improve its product positioning in retail spaces. At the consumer's decision-making point — at the shelf — the product was losing to competitors. Visual branding reinforcement, POS material creation, and development of a shelf placement strategy that would capture consumer attention were needed.

We developed a detailed shelf placement strategy that included visual branding element redesign, POS material design and production (wobblers, shelf talkers, hangers), as well as consumer decision optimization analysis for each retail location.
