ATL Social Media Marketing for a Children's Brand

Dediko Brand was a new children's products brand that needed to build trust with parents on social media. Gaining consumer trust in the children's products market is particularly challenging, as parents are very careful when choosing products for their children. Expert content that would build trust was needed.

We developed a content strategy that included video reels creation, influencer marketing through collaborations with mothers, expert content with pediatricians, a blog post series, email marketing, and social media campaigns. Every piece of content was focused on building trust and providing expert recommendations.
